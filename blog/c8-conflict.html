Conflict

3/8/2015

I'm lucky because I grew up with two lovely little sisters. Conflict definitely came up though, and since we're all adults we are all open to talking about things back then. I think I was pretty emotionally unintelligent as a young kid, but that somehow led to a pretty emotionally fulfilled and emotionally mature adult life. Not sure how that came to be, but I do know it has a lot to do with my family life then.

So speaking of sisters, and speaking of computers one conflict came up when my sister asked to use my computer while I was in college. She was still in high school and didn't quite have one of her own at the time. If I remember correctly, she had been using it for an extraordinarily long amount of time. Unreasonable! But since this is hind-sight, it was probably like 10 minutes. I had just changed majors to Political Science and was openly discussing going into to law school at the time time. After some arguing about the computer, I remember she had said to me "god you'll wind up being a shitty lawyer". I was pissed and then immediately started laughing because, well it was just a good joke at the time. Some times our fights were heated but we often tempered the fights with humor. We often fondly remember cooling off with laughter.

Humor was never used to change the subject during a fight. I think that's important. But making light of a situation when tastefully done can sometimes be pleasant exit.
