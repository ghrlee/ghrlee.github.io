Shine the Chi

Shine the Chi is a mobile-first application for the people of Chicago. It allows Chicagoans to make 311 requests to their city's own 311 API from their smartphone. It gathers user data through google maps to automatically detect and mark the location of a 311 request. Requests are then forwarded to the city to be addressed by local government.
